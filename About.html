Our Story



Koffie Bloem stands as a newly-founded coffeeshop establishment, deeply rooted in its passion for offering an extensive and diverse array of tantalizing food and beverage choices to its esteemed patrons.
Established with a vision and commitment to excellence on the memorable date of August 19, 2021, this esteemed establishment finds its strategic location nestled at the vibrant and easily accessible Jln.Paledang No.38, adjacent to the renowned JNE Paledang.

At Koffie Bloem, patrons can relish an array of delectable food and drink selections, including more than 40+ single origin beans for manual brew. There are also creamy coffees like coffee lattes to a variety of milk-based beverages, enticing mocktails, snacks, and hearty meals.


The meticulously curated menu is thoughtfully designed to cater to an eclectic array of tastes and preferences, ensuring an enriching experience for all who grace this inviting ambiance with their presence.
